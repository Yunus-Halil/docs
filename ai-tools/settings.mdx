## How to go to settings

- On the top right of your screen, click on the profile picture
- Click the **Settings** button

## Regional and Custom Damage Costs

If yuo navigate to the Regional & Pricing tab, you can customize your currency and cost of certain damages. These changes will take place in the project statistics and reports.
